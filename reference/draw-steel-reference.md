# Draw Steel -- Comprehensive Condensed Reference

## 1. Core Mechanics

### Power Rolls

All rolls: **2d10 + characteristic score**. Three tier outcomes:

- **Tier 1**: 11 or lower
- **Tier 2**: 12-16
- **Tier 3**: 17+
- **Natural 19-20**: Always tier 3. On ability rolls with main actions, also a **critical hit** (grants an extra main action immediately).

**Edges and Banes**: Edge = +2, Bane = -2. Double edge (2+ edges, 0 banes) = auto-shift tier up by 1. Double bane (2+ banes, 0 edges) = auto-shift tier down by 1. Edges and banes cancel 1-for-1.

### Characteristics

Five stats (-5 to +5): **Might** (strength), **Agility** (coordination), **Reason** (logic), **Intuition** (instinct), **Presence** (personality). Skills are not tied to characteristics -- any characteristic can theoretically pair with any skill if justified.

### Skills

Five groups with ~10 skills each:

- **Crafting**: Alchemy, Architecture, Blacksmithing, Fletching, Forgery, Jewelry, Mechanics, Tailoring, etc.
- **Exploration**: Climb, Endurance, Gymnastics, Heal, Jump, Lift, Navigate, Ride, Swim, Track
- **Interpersonal**: Brag, Empathize, Flirt, Handle Animals, Interrogate, Intimidate, Lead, Lie, Music, Perform, Persuade
- **Intrigue**: Alertness, Conceal Object, Disguise, Eavesdrop, Escape Artist, Hide, Pick Lock, Pick Pocket, Sabotage, Search, Sneak, Steal
- **Lore**: ~20 domain-specific knowledge skills (Culture, History, Magic, Monsters, Nature, Psionics, Religion, Rumors, Terrain, Timescape, etc.)

Applicable skill grants **+2** to a power roll (stacks with edges). Only one skill per test.

## 2. Ancestries

All ancestries are size 1M, speed 5, stability 0 unless noted. Each has a free signature trait plus ancestry points to spend on purchased traits.

### Devil
- **Signature**: Silver Tongue -- free interpersonal skill, edge to discover NPC motivations/pitfalls.
- **Points**: 3. Traits: Barbed Tail (1), Beast Legs (1, speed 6), Glowing Eyes (1), Hellsight (1), Impressive Horns (2), Prehensile Tail (2), Wings (2).
- **Lore**: Red/blue-skinned humanoids from Seven Cities of Hell. Born with hellmarks (horns, tail, hooves, etc.). Most in Orden are descendants of stranded devils, not visitors.

### Dragon Knight
- **Signature**: Wyrmplate -- damage immunity equal to level to one type (acid/cold/corruption/fire/lightning/poison), changeable at respite.
- **Points**: 3. Traits: Draconian Guard (1), Draconian Pride (2, AoE roar), Dragon Breath (2, cone attack), Prismatic Scales (1), Remember Your Oath (1), Wings (2).
- **Lore**: Created via Dracogenesis ritual. Largest population was the Dragon Phalanx of Vasloria, destroyed by Ajax. Symbols of justice now in exile.

### Dwarf
- **Signature**: Runic Carving -- carve one of three runes (Detection, Light, Voice/telepathy) with 10 min work.
- **Points**: 3. Traits: Great Fortitude (2, immune to weakened), Grounded (1, +1 stability), Spark Off Your Skin (2, +6 Stamina scaling), Stand Tough (1), Stone Singer (1, reshape stone).
- **Lore**: Silico-organic stone flesh. Children of elder god Ord. Dense, strong, long-lived (700-1500 years). Savvy engineers.

### Wode Elf
- **Signature**: Wode Elf Glamor -- edge on hide/sneak tests; bane on searches against you.
- **Points**: 3. Traits: Forest Walk (1), Quick and Brutal (1), Otherworldly Grace (2), Revisit Memory (1), Swift (1), The Wode Defends (2).
- **Lore**: Sylvan forest guardians. Masters of camouflage. Serve Queen Imyrr. Tense relations with high elves.

### High Elf
- **Signature**: High Elf Glamor -- edge on Presence tests (Flirt/Persuade). Appear differently to each viewer.
- **Points**: 3. Traits: Glamor of Terror (2), Graceful Retreat (1), High Senses (1), Otherworldly Grace (2), Revisit Memory (1), Unstoppable Mind (2).
- **Lore**: Created by solar celestials as librarians. Live in fallen sky cities. Devoted to art and beauty. Increasingly insular.

### Hakaan
- **Signature**: Big! -- Size 1L.
- **Points**: 3. Traits: All Is a Feather (1), Doomsight (2), Forceful (1), Great Fortitude (2), Stand Tough (1).
- **Lore**: Descended from Vanigar giants. Traded size for foresight via trickster god. Cursed with "Doomsight" (visions of own death). 9-10 feet tall, stone-like bodies.

### Human
- **Signature**: Detect the Supernatural -- maneuver to detect undead, constructs, extraplanar creatures, supernatural objects within 5 squares.
- **Points**: 3. Traits: Can't Take Hold (1), Determination (2), Perseverance (1), Resist the Unnatural (1), Staying Power (2).
- **Lore**: Uniquely connected to the natural world. Can sense magic. Short-lived but driven. Historically led every coalition against great evils.

### Memonek
- **Signatures**: Fall Lightly (reduce fall by 2 squares) + Lightweight (one size smaller for forced movement).
- **Points**: 4. Traits: I Am Law (1), Keeper of Order (2), Lightning Nimbleness (2), Nonstop (2), Systematic Mind (1), Unphased (1), Useful Emotion (1).
- **Lore**: Native to Axiom (Plane of Law). Silicone/porcelain bodies, creatures of reason. Suffer "velloparatha" (emotional overwhelm) on lower planes.

### Orc
- **Signature**: Relentless -- when damage drops you to dying, make a free strike; if it drops the target to 0, spend a Recovery.
- **Points**: 3. Traits: Bloodfire Rush (1), Glowing Recovery (2), Grounded (1), Nonstop (2), Passionate Artisan (1).
- **Lore**: Fifth speaking people. Peace-loving forest dwellers with inner "bloodfire" that makes veins glow when wounded. Settled borderlands.

### Polder
- **Signatures**: Shadowmeld (maneuver to flatten into shadow, hidden, can't move/act) + Small! (Size 1S).
- **Points**: 4. Traits: Corruption Immunity (1), Fearless (2), Graceful Retreat (1), Nimblestep (2), Polder Geist (1), Reactive Tumble (1).
- **Lore**: ~3.5 ft tall. Shadow-melding ability. Reputation as spies/thieves (also famed chefs). Fiercely independent.

### Revenant
- **Signatures**: Former Life (choose previous ancestry for size) + Tough But Withered (multi-element immunity, fire weakness 5, no food/air/drink needed, go inert instead of dying).
- **Points**: 2 (or 3 if size 1S). Traits: Bloodless (2), Previous Life: 1pt (1), Previous Life: 2pt (2), Undead Influence (1), Vengeance Mark (2).
- **Lore**: Undead risen through unjust death + burning vengeance. Retain memories and personality.

### Time Raider
- **Signature**: Psychic Scar -- psychic immunity equal to level.
- **Points**: 3. Traits: Beyondsight (1), Foresight (1), Four-Armed Athletics (1), Four-Armed Martial Arts (2), Psionic Gift (2), Unstoppable Mind (2).
- **Lore**: Kuran'zoi. Former servitors of evil synliroi psions, self-liberated. Nomads of the timescape. Four arms, crystalline eyes. Loathe authority.

## 3. Background

### Culture
Four aspects that each grant mechanical benefits:

- **Language**: One bonus language beyond Caelian (common tongue).
- **Environment** (Nomadic/Rural/Secluded/Urban/Wilderness): One skill from specified groups.
- **Organization** (Bureaucratic/Communal): One skill from specified groups.
- **Upbringing** (Academic/Creative/Labor/Lawless/Martial/Noble): One skill from specified groups.

Also grants an edge on tests to recall lore about your culture's people.

### Career
Pre-hero vocation. Grants: 2-3 skills, 0-2 languages, possibly +1 Renown, possibly +1 Wealth, possibly project points (120 or 240), and one perk of a specified type. Each career includes a d6 inciting incident table.

Careers include: Agent, Aristocrat, Artisan, Beggar, Criminal, Disciple, Explorer, Farmer, Gladiator, Laborer, Magestic (magic user), Performer, Sage, Soldier, and more.

## 4. Classes

Each class follows a common structure: starting characteristics, Stamina formula, Recoveries, subclass choice, Heroic Resource mechanic, kit selection, signature abilities (at-will), heroic abilities (resource-costed at escalating tiers), and subclass-specific features/abilities layered across 10 levels.

All classes gain an epic resource at level 10 that persists between encounters and converts to their heroic resource.

### Censor (Divine Warrior/Judge)
- **Resource**: Wrath (gain 2/turn, +1 when damaging judged creatures)
- **Core mechanic**: Judgment -- mark an enemy (ranged 10). Judged targets take holy damage when they use main actions. On kill, judgment transfers. Spend wrath for additional triggered effects (prevent shifting, impose bane, taunt).
- **Subclasses**: Exorcist (anti-evil specialist), Oracle (prescient diviner), Paragon (aura commander)
- **Domains**: Choose one from deity's portfolio (12 total). Grants features at levels 1, 4, 7.
- **Stats**: Might 2, Presence 2. Stamina 21 (+9/level). 12 Recoveries.
- **Role**: Melee striker/defender hybrid. Singles out priority targets.

### Conduit (Divine Healer/Caster)
- **Resource**: Piety (roll 1d3/turn; can pray for more at risk of psychic backlash)
- **Core mechanic**: Healing Grace -- ranged 10 maneuver, target spends a Recovery. Spend piety to add targets, clear conditions, grant extra Recoveries.
- **Subclasses**: None traditional. Choose TWO domains from deity's portfolio, creating combinatorial variety.
- **Prayer mechanic**: Before piety roll, can pray: d3 result of 1 = +1 piety but take psychic damage; 2 = +1 piety; 3 = +2 piety + domain prayer effect.
- **Stats**: Intuition 2. Stamina 18 (+6/level). 10 Recoveries.
- **Role**: Primary healer and ranged support. Holy/corruption damage dealer.

### Elementalist (Elemental Mage)
- **Resource**: Essence (gain 2/turn, +1 when nearby creatures take typed damage)
- **Core mechanic**: Seven elements (Air, Earth, Fire, Green, Rot, Void, Water). **Persistent Magic** -- some abilities have ongoing costs that reduce essence gain per turn.
- **Subclasses**: Earth (durability, walls, stability), Fire (pure destruction, ignore fire immunity), Green (healing, shapeshifting into 16 animal forms), Void (teleportation, portals, incorporeality)
- **Key features**: Hurl Element (free strike with chooseable damage type), Practical Magic (utility maneuver), Wyrding (6th level freeform utility magic).
- **Stats**: Reason 2. Stamina 18 (+6/level). 8 Recoveries.
- **Role**: Versatile ranged caster. Can specialize or mix elements freely.

### Fury (Primal Berserker)
- **Resource**: Ferocity (1d3/turn, +1 when taking damage, +1d3 when first winded/dying)
- **Core mechanic**: Growing Ferocity -- tiered passive bonuses at thresholds (2/4/6/8/10/12). Rewards building high ferocity before spending.
- **Subclasses**: Berserker (raw Might, push/collision), Reaver (Agility, slide/cunning), Stormwight (shapeshifter with animal/hybrid forms, uses special kits: Boren/bear, Corven/crow, Raden/rat, Vuken/wolf)
- **Key features**: Mighty Leaps (guaranteed tier 2 on jumps), Primordial Strike (spend ferocity for elemental surge damage).
- **Stats**: Might 2, Agility 2. Stamina 21 (+9/level). 10 Recoveries.
- **Role**: Aggressive melee striker. High durability, heavy forced movement.

### Null (Psionic Anti-Magic Monk)
- **Resource**: Discipline (gain 2/turn, +1 when enemies in Null Field use main actions, +1 when Director spends Malice)
- **Core mechanic**: Null Field -- persistent 1-aura that reduces enemy potencies. Spend discipline for additional field effects (gravity disruption, inertia anchoring, synaptic interference). Many abilities permanently expand the field.
- **Subclasses**: Chronokinetic (temporal, speed, Disengage bonuses), Cryokinetic (cold, grab synergy), Metakinetic (gravity/force, Knockback synergy)
- **Key features**: Inertial Shield (triggered half-damage), Psionic Martial Arts (use Intuition for Knockback/Grab), Discipline Mastery (tiered passives like Growing Ferocity).
- **Stats**: Agility 2, Intuition 2. Stamina 21 (+9/level). 8 Recoveries.
- **Role**: Melee controller/defender. Anti-caster specialist. Suppresses enemy abilities.

### Shadow (Assassin/Infiltrator)
- **Resource**: Insight (1d3/turn, +1 when dealing surge damage; costs -1 if you have edge/double edge)
- **Core mechanic**: Surge economy. Signature abilities and college features generate surges constantly. Surges convert to extra damage and feed insight generation.
- **Subclasses**: College of Black Ash (teleportation sorcery), College of Caustic Alchemy (acids, bombs, poisons), College of the Harlequin Mask (illusion magic, deception)
- **Key features**: Hesitation Is Weakness (spend 1 insight to act immediately after another hero), Careful Observation (maneuver for edge + surges), Umbral Form (shadow creature transformation at 6th level).
- **Stats**: Agility 2 + one other at 2. Stamina 18 (+6/level). 8 Recoveries. 7 skills (most of any class).
- **Role**: High single-target damage dealer. Mobile, evasive. Excels at turn-order manipulation.

### Tactician (Battlefield Commander)
- **Resource**: Focus (gain 2/turn, +1 when ally damages marked creature, +1 when ally uses heroic ability)
- **Core mechanic**: Mark (maneuver, ranged 10) -- marked enemies grant edge to tactician and nearby allies. On hit, spend 1 focus for bonus damage, ally Recovery, ally shift, or taunt. Strike Now lets allies use signature abilities on your turn.
- **Subclasses**: Insurgent (subterfuge, forced friendly fire), Mastermind (multi-mark, info warfare), Vanguard (front-line, parry, charge enhancement)
- **Key features**: Field Arsenal (equip two kits simultaneously), Grandmaster of Arms (auto tier 3 on signatures at 9th), Warmaster (allies roll 3d10 choose 2 vs marked targets at 10th).
- **Stats**: Might and one other. Stamina 21 (+9/level). 10 Recoveries.
- **Role**: Force multiplier. Makes allies better. Marks priority targets.

### Talent (Psionic Powerhouse)
- **Resource**: Clarity (1d3/turn, +1 when creatures are force moved). **Can go negative** -- negative clarity = "strained," taking self-damage each turn. Many abilities have bonus Strained effects that are stronger but self-harmful.
- **Core mechanic**: Risk vs. reward tension. Push into negative clarity for powerful strained effects at the cost of self-damage and conditions.
- **Subclasses**: Chronopathy (time manipulation, rerolls, dilation), Telekinesis (force/movement, gravity), Telepathy (mind reading/control, psychic damage)
- **Key features**: Mind Spike (built-in ranged free strike), Psionic Augmentation (passive buff: density/force/speed/distance/armor), Psi Boost (spend extra clarity for modular enhancements to any ability).
- **Stats**: Reason 2, plus one other. Stamina 18 (+6/level). 8 Recoveries.
- **Role**: Versatile ranged/control. Risk-reward playstyle. High ceiling when pushing limits.

### Troubadour (Performance-Based Support)
- **Resource**: Drama (1d3/turn, +2 when 3 heroes act on same turn, +2 when any hero goes winded, +3 on any natural 19-20, +10 on hero death). At 30 drama while dead, you resurrect.
- **Core mechanic**: Routines (Performances) -- choose a persistent aura each round that buffs allies (speed boost, Recovery spending, subclass-specific effects). Scene Partner lets you bond with NPCs for negotiation bonuses.
- **Subclasses**: Auteur (narrative manipulation, teleport repositioning, "rewrite the story"), Duelist (melee combat dancer, riposte, one-on-one focus), Virtuoso (musical magic, sonic damage, persistent song auras)
- **Key features**: Drama generation from battlefield drama (winding, crits, deaths). Appeal to the Muses (boost drama gain at risk of granting Director Malice).
- **Stats**: Agility 2, Presence 2. Stamina 18 (+6/level). 8 Recoveries.
- **Role**: Support/controller. Buff allies, debuff enemies, reactive to dramatic swings.

## 6. Perks and Complications

### Perks
Non-combat features. One from career, more from class advancement. Six types: Crafting, Exploration, Interpersonal, Intrigue, Lore, Supernatural. Having a perk lets you do something without a test or better than a test would normally allow.

### Complications (Optional)
100 backstory twists (selectable or d100). Each grants both a benefit and a drawback of roughly equal weight. Examples: Cursed Weapon (leveled magic weapon + damage weakness), Feytouched (+1 heroic resource at combat start, Director gains 3 Malice), Lifebonded (respawn on death, but die if bonded creature dies).

## 7. Tests

### Difficulty Scaling

| Roll | Easy | Medium | Hard |
|------|------|--------|------|
| <=11 | Success w/ consequence | Failure | Failure w/ consequence |
| 12-16 | Success | Success w/ consequence | Failure |
| 17+ | Success w/ reward | Success | Success |

### Test Types
- **Standard test**: One hero rolls. Director sets difficulty and relevant characteristic.
- **Assist a Test**: Maneuver. Roll your own test. Tier 1 = bane to assisted; tier 2 = edge; tier 3 = double edge.
- **Opposed Power Roll**: Both sides roll, highest total wins. No tiers.
- **Reactive Test**: Director calls for a roll without context (detect hidden things, etc.).
- **Group Test**: Everyone rolls. Succeeds if half or more succeed.
- **Hero Tokens**: Spend to reroll any test.

### Montage Tests
Multi-round collaborative skill challenges (2 rounds default). Each hero acts once per round: test, assist, or use an ability. Can't reuse the same skill. Director sets success/failure limits. Three outcomes: total success, partial success, total failure.

## 8. Combat

### Turn Structure
Alternating activation. No initiative. Roll d10 at start: 6+ = players choose who goes first. Sides alternate turns. One side runs out, the other takes remaining turns consecutively. First-round order persists.

### Actions Per Turn
- **Main action**: Class abilities, Charge (move + melee free strike), Defend (double bane on attacks vs you), Free Strike, Heal (adjacent ally spends Recovery or makes save).
- **Maneuver**: Aid Attack (edge to ally), Catch Breath (spend Recovery), Escape Grab, Grab, Hide, Knockback (push 1-3), Stand Up, Use Consumable, Make/Assist a Test.
- **Move action**: Advance (move up to speed) or Disengage (shift 1).
- **Triggered action**: One per round, on any turn, when a specific trigger occurs. Free triggered actions don't count against this.

### Movement
Standard speed 5. Movement types: walk, climb (2:1 without climb speed), burrow, swim, crawl (2:1), fly, hover, teleport, shift (no opportunity attacks). Difficult terrain costs +1 to enter. High ground grants edge.

### Forced Movement
Push (away), Pull (toward), Slide (any direction). Stability reduces distance. Slamming into creatures deals 1 damage per remaining square. Slamming into solid objects deals 2 + 1 per remaining square.

### Stamina and Death
- **Winded**: At half Stamina max or below. Many abilities trigger off this.
- **Dying**: At 0 Stamina or below. Automatically bleeding. Can still act. Can't Catch Breath.
- **Death**: Stamina reaches negative of winded value.
- **Recovery value**: 1/3 of Stamina max. Recoveries are a limited pool.
- **Temporary Stamina**: Absorbs damage first, doesn't stack (take higher), expires end of encounter.
- **Saving throws**: Roll d10; 6+ ends a "save ends" effect.

### Conditions
- **Bleeding**: Take 1d6 + level damage on main/triggered actions or Might/Agility power rolls.
- **Dazed**: Only one of main action, maneuver, or move action. No triggered/free actions.
- **Frightened**: Bane on abilities vs source; can't approach source; source has edge vs you.
- **Grabbed**: Speed 0; bane on abilities not targeting grabber.
- **Prone**: Bane on own strikes; melee attacks vs you gain edge.
- **Restrained**: Speed 0; can't stand up or be force moved.
- **Slowed**: Speed reduced to 2.
- **Taunted**: Bane on abilities not targeting taunter.
- **Weakened**: Abilities deal half damage; can't gain surges.

## 9. Negotiation

Structured social encounters with tracked stats:

- **Interest** (0-5): NPC's willingness to deal. 5 = best deal, 4 = full deal, 3 = deal with catch, 2 = partial, 1 = rejection, 0 = hostile.
- **Patience** (0-5): How many arguments the NPC will hear. Drops by 1 per argument. At 0, NPC makes final offer.
- **Motivations** (12 types): Arguments matching an unused motivation get easier tests. Each usable once.
- **Pitfalls** (same 12 categories): Triggering one auto-fails and drops Interest and Patience by 1.
- **Motivations/Pitfalls**: Benevolence, Discovery, Freedom, Greed, Higher Authority, Justice, Legacy, Peace, Power, Protection, Revelry, Vengeance.
- **Renown/Impression**: If hero's Renown >= NPC's Impression score, hero gains edges on certain interpersonal tests.

## 10. Downtime and Respites

A **respite** is a 24-hour rest period. Heroes regain all Stamina and Recoveries. Victories convert to XP. One respite activity allowed:

### Downtime Projects
Make **project rolls** (characteristic + skill). Total accrues as project points toward a goal. Natural 19-20 = breakthrough (+20 points + another roll).

**Crafting projects**: Build Airship (3000), Craft Teleportation Platform (1500), Imbue Treasure (150 per enhancement tier at levels 1/5/9), Find a Cure, Build Road.

**Research projects**: Discover Lore (15/45/120/240 by rarity), Go Undercover (15), Learn From a Master (120-1000), Learn New Language.

Prerequisites: Item prerequisite (component) + project source (book/tutor) in a specific language.

## 11. Rewards

### Treasures
- **Consumables**: Single-use items gated by echelon (potions, dusts, oils).
- **Trinkets**: Permanent supernatural items (cloaks, boots, bags, etc.).
- **Leveled Treasures**: Imbued armor/implements/weapons at 1st/5th/9th level tiers. Grant Stamina (+6/+12/+21 for armor) or damage (+1/+2/+3 for weapons/implements) bonuses.
- **Artifacts**: Campaign-defining unique items. Intentionally unbalanced.

### Titles
Earned through deeds, assigned to echelons (1st-4th). Each offers 2-4 mechanical benefit choices. Examples: Brawler, Faction Member, Doomed, Dragon Blooded, Monarch, Demigod.

### Renown
Fame score starting at 0, ~+1 per level. Influences negotiations via Impression scores. Attracts followers at thresholds (3/6/9/12 = 1/2/3/4 followers). Follower types: Artisans (crafting), Sages (research), Retainers (combat NPCs).

### Wealth
Abstract score 1-6. Heroes start at 1, gain ~1 every 2 levels. Magic items cannot be purchased.

## 12. Gods and Religion

Gods are real but **forbidden from acting directly** in the world. They work through **saints** -- once-mortal demigod intermediaries who can briefly manifest. Churches are consecrated to saints. Multiple churches can serve the same saint with different interpretations.

### Domains (12 total)
Creation, Death, Fate, Knowledge, Life, Love, Nature, Protection, Storm, Sun, Trickery, War. Used by Censors (pick 1) and Conduits (pick 2).

### Elder Gods
- **Val** (elves): Creation, Knowledge, Life, Nature, Protection. Values art and beauty.
- **Ord** (dwarves): Creation, Knowledge, Protection, Sun, War. Values integrity, honor, creation.
- **Kul** (orcs): Knowledge, Life, Sun, Trickery, War. Fire that destroys and creates. Dislikes worship.

Hakaan are animists (nature spirits, ancestor reverence). Their heroes become constellations.

### Saint-to-Conduit Pathway
Conduits/Censors progress from ritual orisons to personal saint relationships to sensing the god behind the saint. The pathway from high-level conduit to sainthood was once known but is now obscure.

## 13. Progression and Director Guidance

### Levels and Echelons
- **1st echelon** (levels 1-3): Local heroes
- **2nd echelon** (levels 4-6): Regional heroes
- **3rd echelon** (levels 7-9): World-shaping heroes
- **4th echelon** (level 10): Legendary/demigod-tier heroes

### Reward Cadence
- Victories: 1 per combat/montage/negotiation success; 2 for hard encounters.
- Treasures: 2 leveled per hero by 10th level; 1 trinket per hero per echelon; 1-3 consumables per level.
- Titles: ~1 every other level.
- Renown: ~1 per level.
- Wealth: ~1 every 2 levels.

### Setting
- **Orden**: The prime world. Vasloria (medieval fantasy), Capital (cosmopolitan city of intrigue).
- **The Timescape**: The multiverse -- Seven Cities of Hell, Axiom, wodes, and more.
- **Ajax**: Central villain. Tyrant who destroyed the Dragon Phalanx and conquered Vasloria.
